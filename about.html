About AuraCrafts

Built by makers, for makers — empowering creators worldwide

Our Story

AuraCrafts was born from a simple idea: making 3D printing accessible, inspiring, and profitable for every creator — from bedroom hobbyists to professional product designers.

We curate the best STL files, recommend trusted printers and materials, and connect makers with professional manufacturing services so you can spend less time searching and more time creating.

Every design in our catalog comes with a commercial license, so you can use your prints to start or grow a business — sell at craft fairs, on Etsy, or to local customers.
