Improving FDM Filament Drying With a Spot of Vacuum

Ensuring your 3D printing filament is properly dried is crucial for achieving the best prints, and now, a simple addition of a vacuum to your drying process could be the solution. This innovative method, showcased in a recent article on Hackaday (Read the original article), demonstrates how vacuuming can significantly speed up and improve the drying process, leaving filament ready for use with minimal moisture content.

While traditional filament dryers are effective, the vacuum technique offers a fresh perspective. By using a vacuum to draw out moisture from the filament, this method promises both faster drying times and better filament integrity. The vacuum’s ability to create a more controlled and consistent drying environment could be a game-changer for 3D printers, particularly in environments where space and energy efficiency are critical.
