A Status Screen For Bambu Labs Printers

May 18, 2026

Headline: A Status Screen For Bambu Labs Printers

Article: If you own a Bambu Labs printer, keeping tabs on its progress has become significantly easier with the introduction of a dedicated status screen. This new feature provides a seamless way to monitor the printer’s performance, whether through the on-board display or the app suite that the company has developed.

Feature: The addition of this status screen offers a more comprehensive view of the printer’s status, making it a valuable tool for users looking to ensure their printing projects are progressing as expected. With this new interface, users can keep a closer watch on the printer’s output, helping to streamline the printing process and potentially reducing errors or delays.
