Modular Mechanical Keyboard Turned into Compact Workstation with 3D Printing

3D printing continues to expand its capabilities beyond just creating decorative items. [anurag.id] highlights a unique project where a classic mechanical keyboard has been transformed into a compact workstation. This innovative use of 3D printing demonstrates how versatile this technology is, capable of turning everyday objects into multifunctional tools.

By combining the keyboard's structure with other components, it's possible to create a portable setup that integrates seamlessly into daily tasks. This project not only showcases the creativity within the hacker community but also the potential of 3D printing to transform simple items into essential accessories for a modern workspace.
