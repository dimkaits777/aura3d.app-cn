3D Printed Orrery Runs On A Single Motor

Here’s a remarkable 3D printed orrery that runs on a single motor, making it an excellent model for observing the motion of the solar system

The device provides a detailed and dynamic model of our solar system, allowing users to see the movements of planets and moons in motion

It’s a perfect example of how advanced 3D printing technology can be used to create intricate and functional models that offer educational insights into the cosmos
