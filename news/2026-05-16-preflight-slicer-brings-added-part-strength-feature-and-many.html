preFlight Slicer Brings Added Part Strength Feature, and Many More

Interested in taking some wild new 3D printing features for a test drive? preFlight is a free and open source slicer that brings a host of processing improvements as well as an added part strength feature.

  • preFlight offers a unique feature that enhances part strength, making it a standout choice for those looking to improve their 3D printing projects.
  • Alongside the strength enhancement, preFlight introduces several other improvements, including a more robust user interface and better compatibility with a variety of 3D printers and filament types.
