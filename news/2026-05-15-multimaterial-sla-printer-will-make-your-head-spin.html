Multimaterial SLA Printer Will Make Your Head Spin

May 15, 2026

3D Printing News: Multimaterial SLA Printer

Companies have been vying to showcase multi-material printing as a must-have feature on their filament deposition 3D printers for years. Now, a new development is set to leave even the most tech-savvy among us spinning.

  • Imagine a multimaterial SLA printer that can seamlessly handle multiple materials at once, challenging the limits of conventional technology and opening up new possibilities for rapid prototyping and production.
  • What makes this device particularly exciting is its ability to blend and transition between different materials, providing an unprecedented level of customization and versatility in a single print. This is set to revolutionize the way we approach material integration in 3D printing.
