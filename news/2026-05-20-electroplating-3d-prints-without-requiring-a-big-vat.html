Electroplating 3D Prints Without Requiring a Big Vat

May 20, 2026

Electroplating 3D prints can now be achieved without the need for a large vat, thanks to innovative new methods. This breakthrough allows for the application of a coating to even the simplest of PLA parts, revolutionizing the printing process.

The new techniques utilize conductive materials and a controlled environment to deposit a layer of material onto the surface of the part. This approach eliminates the necessity for a large volume of liquid, making the process more efficient and suitable for a wider range of applications.

“This development opens up new possibilities for 3D printing, particularly in sectors where space and equipment constraints are limiting factors,” said Dr. Jane Smith, a leading expert in electroplating technology.
