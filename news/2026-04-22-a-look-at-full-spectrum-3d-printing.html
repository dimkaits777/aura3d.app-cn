A Look at Full Spectrum 3D Printing

April 22, 2026

Full Spectrum 3D Printing Takes Color Printing to the Next Level

Explore the revolutionary advancements in 3D printing with full spectrum capabilities. This breakthrough allows for the creation of objects with a wide range of colors and materials in a single build, revolutionizing how designers and manufacturers approach color application in 3D models.

Traditional 3D printers often limit the number of colors used in a single print, often just a few, which can be limiting for intricate designs. Full spectrum 3D printing, however, enables the creation of complex, multi-colored designs with a single print run, offering unmatched flexibility and efficiency.
