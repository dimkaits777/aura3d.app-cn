3D Printed Orrery Runs On A Single Motor

The solar system is kind of hard to observe in motion all at once. Sometimes, it’s nice to have a little model to look at, so you can see the planets orbiting each other.

  • Now, a 3D printed orrery is available that runs on a single motor, making it even easier to understand the complex movements of the solar system.
  • Developed by a team of engineers, the orrery includes all the planets and moons, and their relative positions, all revolving around the sun as if in a real solar system.
