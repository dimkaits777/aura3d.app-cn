May 15, 2026

preFlight Slicer Brings Added Part Strength Feature, and Many More Read the original article

Improved Part Strength

The preFlight Slicer introduces a feature that enhances the strength of the printed parts, making it a standout feature for enthusiasts and professionals alike. This improvement comes with a suite of processing improvements that make the slicing process more efficient and reliable.

More Features

  • preFlight also offers a wide range of other features including automated calibration and advanced slicing techniques that can reduce layer height, speed, and infill density for optimal results.
  • The open-source nature of the slicer means that users can contribute to its development, which not only encourages collaboration but also ensures that future updates and improvements are made based on user feedback.
