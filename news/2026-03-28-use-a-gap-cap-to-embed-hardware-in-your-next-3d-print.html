Use a Gap-Cap to Embed Hardware In Your Next 3D Print

Learn how to tackle the challenge of embedding fasteners or other hardware directly into your 3D prints, especially for large or non-flat objects. A gap-cap is the perfect solution.

  • Gap-caps offer a simple and effective way to incorporate hardware into your prints without compromising the design's integrity or the print's surface quality.
  • They are particularly useful for projects requiring precision and durability, such as building mechanical parts or adding electronic components.
  • With a gap-cap, you can ensure that your hardware is flush with the print's surface, making it both aesthetically pleasing and functional.
