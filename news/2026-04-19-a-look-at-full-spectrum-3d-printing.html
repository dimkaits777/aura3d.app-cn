Enhancing the Color Palette

While many modern desktop 3D printers offer the capability to print in multiple colors, the implementation often restricts users to a limited palette of hues at once. This limitation poses a significant barrier for designers and manufacturers aiming to create intricate and vibrant objects that utilize a broader spectrum of colors. Full spectrum 3D printing represents a revolutionary advancement in this field, promising to break down these barriers and allow for the realization of objects with an unparalleled array of colors.
