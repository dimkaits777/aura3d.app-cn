3D Printed Orrery Runs On A Single Motor

The solar system is kind of hard to observe in motion all at once. Sometimes, it’s nice to have a little model to look at, so you can see the planets rotating and orbiting in sync. Researchers have created a 3D printed orrery that uses a single motor to power all the mechanisms. The orrery is designed to be a teaching tool to help people understand the complex movements of the solar system.

  • The orrery features a beautifully detailed 3D printed model of the solar system, including all the planets and their moons.
  • The single motor drives the entire mechanism, which is designed to simulate the rotational and orbital motions of the planets.
  • This innovative orrery not only simplifies understanding the solar system but also serves as a stunning example of 3D printing technology.
