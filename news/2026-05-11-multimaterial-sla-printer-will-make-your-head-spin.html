Multimaterial SLA Printer Will Make Your Head Spin

Read the original article: Source:

  • A new multimaterial SLA (stereolithography) printer has been developed, promising to revolutionize the additive manufacturing landscape.
  • Unlike traditional SLA printers that can only print with one type of material, this innovative device allows for the simultaneous use of multiple materials, which opens up new possibilities for intricate designs and functional components.
  • Developers are excited about the potential for applications in various fields, including electronics, aerospace, and medical devices, where the ability to precisely control the material composition is crucial.
