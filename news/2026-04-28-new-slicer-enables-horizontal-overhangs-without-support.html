New Slicer Enables Horizontal Overhangs Without Support

April 28, 2026

New Slicer Enabling Horizontal Overhangs Without Support

A new slicer has been introduced to the 3D printing world, promising to break the traditional rule of thumb for FDM printing: overhangs can now be achieved at any angle, including horizontal positions, without the need for support structures.

This innovation comes as a relief for designers who often struggle with creating vertical or horizontal overhangs in their designs. The new slicer utilizes advanced algorithms that analyze the design and automatically adjust the layer orientation and infill pattern to optimize the print process, resulting in smoother surfaces and fewer failed prints.
