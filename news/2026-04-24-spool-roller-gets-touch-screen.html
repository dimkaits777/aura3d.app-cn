Spool Roller Gets Touch Screen

Read the original article

  • Desktop 3D printers often need a space for filament spools. [LVTRC] has addressed this with a spool roller.
  • The spool roller from [LVTRC] not only holds spools securely but also includes a scale, adding a practical feature.
  • Enhanced with a touch screen, the spool roller makes it easier to manage and track filament inventory.
