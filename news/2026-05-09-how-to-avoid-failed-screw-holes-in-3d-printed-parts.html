How To Avoid Failed Screw Holes In 3D Printed Parts

Ensure Proper Hole Diameter and Depth for Successful Screw Insertion.

  • Choose the Right Hole Size
  • Balance Strength and Precision
  • Check for Proper Depth

Optimize Hole Positioning for Better Screw Insertion.

  • Align with Screw Axis
  • Prevent Interference with Moving Parts
  • Keep Hole Clear for Easy Access

Prepare the Hole Properly for Enhanced Screw Functionality.

  • Use a Center Drill
  • Smooth the Hole
  • Eliminate Sharp Corners
