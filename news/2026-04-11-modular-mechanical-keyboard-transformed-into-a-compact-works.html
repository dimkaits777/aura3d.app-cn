Modular Mechanical Keyboard Transformed Into a Compact Workstation

April 11, 2026

3D printing has become a cornerstone of the hacker community, capable of transforming nearly anything into a functional object. [anurag.id] highlights a fascinating example where a traditional mechanical keyboard, known for its precision and tactile feedback, was repurposed into a compact workstation.

In this innovative project, the modular design of the keyboard allows for customization and expansion, fitting it perfectly into various spaces and configurations. The resulting setup is not only a testament to the versatility of 3D printing but also an intriguing demonstration of how technology can be creatively adapted to serve multiple purposes.

By turning this everyday device into a compact workstation, the modder showcases the endless possibilities of 3D printing, suggesting that even in a simple item like a keyboard, complex functionalities can be integrated, making it a valuable asset for both home and office environments.
