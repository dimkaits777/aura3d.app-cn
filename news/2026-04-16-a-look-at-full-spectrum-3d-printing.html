Expanding Color Capabilities in 3D Printing

While many desktop 3D printers offer the option to print in multiple colors, the process is often constrained by only a few options, and adding more colors significantly reduces performance. Full-spectrum 3D printing aims to overcome these limitations by enabling the use of a wide range of colors and materials, offering unprecedented creativity and flexibility in design and prototyping.
