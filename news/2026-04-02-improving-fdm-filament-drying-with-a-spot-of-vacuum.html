Improving FDM Filament Drying With a Spot of Vacuum

Advancements in technology continue to enhance the efficiency of filament drying in FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) processes, addressing one of the most critical steps in 3D printing. This innovation aims to reduce the need for prolonged exposure to moisture, which can negatively impact print quality.

  • New methods, including the use of a vacuum, are being explored to expedite the drying process, ensuring the filament remains in a dry state without compromising its integrity.
  • By employing a vacuum, the drying time can be significantly cut, reducing the environmental impact and the need for additional drying steps.
