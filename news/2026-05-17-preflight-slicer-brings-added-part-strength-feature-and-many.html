preFlight Slicer Brings Added Part Strength Feature, and Many More

May 17, 2026

preFlight Slicer Brings Added Part Strength Feature, and Many More

Source: Read the original article

preFlight Slicer, a free and open-source slicing software, introduces new features that promise to revolutionize the 3D printing experience. Besides the notable strength enhancement for printed parts, preFlight also offers several other improvements.

Among these improvements, preFlight now includes a preflight analysis feature that helps users identify potential issues in their 3D models and printing settings before the print begins. Additionally, the software features a more intuitive user interface, enhanced bed leveling, and improved support generation algorithms. These enhancements contribute to a smoother and more efficient printing process.
