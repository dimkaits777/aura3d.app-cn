Improving FDM Filament Drying With a Spot of Vacuum

Enhancing the drying process of FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) filament with a touch of vacuum can significantly improve the quality of 3D prints.

Experts in the field suggest using a filament dryer equipped with a vacuum to prevent moisture exposure, a key factor that can degrade filament quality and lead to unsatisfactory prints.

This innovative method allows for faster drying times and more consistent filament conditions, ensuring better print outcomes and minimizing issues related to moisture contamination.
