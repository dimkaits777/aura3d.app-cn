A Look at Full Spectrum 3D Printing

Full Spectrum 3D Printing: Revolutionizing Color Printing

  • Traditional desktop 3D printers often limit the number of colors used in a single print.
  • A new development in 3D printing technology aims to change that with full spectrum printing capabilities.
  • Full spectrum 3D printing allows for prints in a variety of colors, not just a few, by leveraging the full range of colors available in the visible spectrum.
