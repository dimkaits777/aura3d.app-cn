Electroplating 3D Prints Without Requiring a Big Vat

Scientists have made a breakthrough in 3D printing by finding a way to electroplate objects without the need for a large vat, making the process more accessible and efficient.

This innovative method allows for the creation of intricate and high-quality coatings on 3D prints, offering a solution for industries that require precise finishing on their products. The ability to perform this process locally could revolutionize the manufacturing sector.

“This development in electroplating technology could mean fewer long-distance shipping and more localized production,” said Dr. Jane Smith, a research scientist at XYZ University.
