Spool Roller Gets Touch Screen

April 26, 2026

Spool Roller Gets Touch Screen

LVTRC has unveiled a new version of their spool roller, integrating a touch screen to enhance usability. The original device was designed to hang filament spools, but now it also features a scale.

The touch screen allows users to easily monitor filament levels and control the printing process, improving convenience and efficiency. This upgrade makes the spool roller even more versatile for users of desktop 3D printers.
