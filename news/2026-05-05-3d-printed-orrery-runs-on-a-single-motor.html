3D Printed Orrery Runs On A Single Motor

The solar system is kind of hard to observe in motion all at once. Sometimes, it’s nice to have a little model to look at, so you can see the intricate dance of planets orbiting the sun. A recent advancement in 3D printing has made this possible: a single-motor orrery that captures the motion of the solar system in a compact model.

Created by a creative team, this 3D printed orrery is a remarkable piece of engineering. Not only does it represent the solar system with precision, but it does so using only a single motor to power the rotation of the planets and the sun. This innovative design allows for a compact, yet lifelike, representation of our celestial neighborhood.
