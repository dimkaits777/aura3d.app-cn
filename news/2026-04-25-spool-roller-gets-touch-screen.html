Spool Roller Gets Touch Screen

April 25, 2026

Spool Roller Gets Touch Screen

LVTRC has upgraded its popular spool roller to include a touch screen interface. This innovative device now caters to desktop 3D printer enthusiasts looking for a convenient way to manage their filament supply. The new feature allows users to easily adjust settings and monitor the scale directly from the touch screen, enhancing the user experience.

With the touch screen integration, the spool roller not only simplifies the process of managing filament spools but also offers a more intuitive control system. Users can navigate through various functionalities effortlessly, making it easier to maintain their 3D printing process efficiently. The addition of a touch screen not only brings modern technology to the classic filament management tool but also makes it more accessible and user-friendly for a broader audience.
