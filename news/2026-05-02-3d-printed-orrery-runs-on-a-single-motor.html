3D Printed Orrery Runs On A Single Motor

The solar system is indeed fascinating, and often difficult to observe in motion all at once. Sometimes, having a smaller model that showcases the system in action can be very helpful for understanding the complexities of celestial bodies.

Recently, a creative individual has taken on this challenge by creating a solar system orrery that operates using a single motor. This innovative design not only simplifies the observation of planetary movements but also makes it more accessible to a wider audience, including students and enthusiasts looking to enhance their learning experiences.
