Optimizing Print Speed for ABS Resin

Tips for maximizing print speed on a Da Vinci Neon 3D printer using ABS resin, including layer height, infill density, and cooling.

April 17, 2026

Optimizing Print Speed for ABS Resin with Da Vinci Neon 3D Printer

When using a Da Vinci Neon 3D printer for ABS resin prints, achieving optimal print speed is crucial to ensuring quality and efficiency. This article aims to provide insights and tips to help you maximize your print speed while maintaining the desired outcome of your prints.

To ensure your ABS resin prints are both fast and smooth, it's important to set the right parameters within your 3D printing software. One of the key adjustments is the layer height, which should be reduced for faster print speeds. Lower layer heights allow for faster print speeds without compromising the final print quality.

A lower layer height means each layer of the print is thinner, resulting in less time spent on each layer. However, keep in mind that thinner layers also require a finer nozzle and possibly a more precise extruder to maintain control. Ensure your extruder is properly calibrated to maintain consistent filament extrusion.

Another critical factor for optimizing print speed is the infill density. A lower infill density can significantly increase print speed, especially if you're printing with a high layer height. Reducing the infill density involves adjusting the infill pattern and the percentage of infill. Start by lowering the infill percentage and experiment with different infill patterns to see what works best for your specific project.

To further optimize print speed, consider the type of extruder you are using. Some extruders are designed for higher speeds and may offer better performance at higher speeds compared to others. For instance, if you are using a direct drive extruder, you may be able to achieve higher speeds without as many adjustments compared to a belt driven extruder.

Additionally, cooling can play a significant role in achieving a faster print speed. Proper cooling helps in preventing warping and ensures that the print maintains a uniform shape throughout the entire printing process. Ensure that your cooling system is correctly set up to handle the specific temperature requirements of your ABS resin.

Material selection is also important for achieving fast print speeds. Some resins, like ABS, are known for their faster print speeds compared to others, such as PLA. However, when using a higher layer height, the print speed can be improved, making ABS still a viable option for speed optimization.

Another helpful tip is to monitor your print speed using the Da Vinci Neon's in-built diagnostics and monitor the temperature of your extruder, hot end, and ambient temperature. Adjustments based on these readings can help in fine-tuning the print settings for optimal results.

Lastly, regular maintenance of your Da Vinci Neon 3D printer, including cleaning the nozzle and checking for blockages, is essential to maintaining consistent performance. A clean print environment also contributes to better print speed and quality.

By implementing these strategies, you can optimize your print speed for ABS resin on the Da Vinci Neon 3D printer. Remember to experiment with different settings to find the optimal balance between speed and quality for your specific prints.
