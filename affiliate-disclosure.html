Last Updated: March 1, 2026 | Effective: January 1, 2026

FTC Disclosure Compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 C.F.R. Part 255), AuraCrafts discloses that our website contains affiliate links. This means that if you click on a link to a product or service and make a purchase, AuraCrafts may receive a commission or other compensation.

This disclosure applies to all content published on aura3d.app-cn.net, including but not limited to product listings, reviews, blog posts, and recommendations.

Our Editorial Policy

Our affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial content. We only recommend products and services that we genuinely believe provide value to our audience. Compensation from affiliate programs does not affect which products we feature, the ratings we assign, or the conclusions we reach in our reviews.

We strive to be honest and transparent in all our content. If we have a material connection to a product or service beyond an affiliate relationship (such as receiving a free product for review), we will disclose that as well.

No Additional Cost to You

When you purchase through our affiliate links, the price you pay is identical to buying directly. In some cases, our affiliate partnerships allow us to offer you exclusive discounts or promotions that you would not otherwise receive.
